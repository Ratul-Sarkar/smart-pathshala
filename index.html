Our Story

In a world where learning only comes from our formative years in school and college, Smart Pathshala is the new, easy, and definitive path for keeping up with the 21st century. It is the platform where you can finally stop trying to fit in a system crafted for the masses and learn in a unique way that brings out the best version of YOU!

A Major Fact !

Do you know that by the year 2025, the youth population in India is going to 600 million, which is 45% of the total population? Now, this means a great deal for the growth and prosperity of the nation and as a young country, India is destined to be in it’s most progressive and economically promising state. However, a statistic that’s equally disturbing is that less than 50% of these 600 million youth are going to be employable. What does this say about the nation’s growth and empowerment of the people?
